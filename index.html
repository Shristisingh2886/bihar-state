Guidelines for covid in 2021



According to bihar health department (because of the second phase of corona) lockdown for 30 days will be there.
                                                                                                                            Because of corona cases are increasing our cheif minister and health department has decided to make a lockdown till 1may 2021.
In the lockdown every schools and offices will be closed, only banks, grocery shops, pharmacies, and hospitals will be open. Not a single government transport will be running.No one should step outside from there homes.In case of emergency only one should move outside from there homes, in every street there will be a team of police officers everyone have to take permission from them then only one can go outside from the house.At once only four people can go outfrom the house.While you are outside you have to carry your own mask, hand sanitizer and gloves, and you have to maintain social distaincing.
